Set up immunoprecipitation or DNA fishing.

Currently experimenting with pre-absorbtion on column. Run lysate through column.

Add 1 µL antibody and incubate for at least 1 hour or overnight (at 4°C) with rotation.

Add 50-100 µL protein A or streptavidin magnetic beads - incubate for at least 30 minutes at 4°C.

Running and eluting columns

place a µMACs column in the magnet -- wash each column with 200 µL lysis buffer.

columns are flow-stop so they do not run dry.

load lysate onto column in magnet -- collect flow through

Wash column 4X with lysis buffer - 400 µL each. I am trying 2 X lysis buffer, 1 X high salt wash buffer (from immunoprecipitation protocol) and 1 X low salt buffer.

Wash with 100 µL 20mM Tris-HCl pH 7.5

Apply 20 µL hot (95°C) 1X lysis buffer to the column - incubate 5 min. at room temperature

Apply 50 µL hot (95°C) and collect sample.

Run gel.
